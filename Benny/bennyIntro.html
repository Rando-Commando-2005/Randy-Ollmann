Benny's Spaceship Adventure

Welcome to Benny's Spaceship Adventure. Benny and his crew are stuck on the space station
after colliding with asteroid B 612. Their fuel tank was ruptured and aren't able to fly.
Fortunately, the crew managed to safely tuck themselves into an airlock but they are
running out of food and water. You were in the area and recieved the sistress signal. Now it
is your mission to rescue Benny and his crew. All your viewing ports have been blocked by
space pigeon droppings, and you don't have time to do a space walk to clear it off. Good
thing your spaceship has sophistocated instruments and you're an experienced pilot. The
lives of Benny and his crew are in your hands. Good luck and godspeed.
